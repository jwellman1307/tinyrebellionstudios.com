Android budgeting app

SpendGuard

SpendGuard is a paycheck-focused budgeting app that helps you quickly see how much money is safe to spend before your next paycheck.

It is built for people who want a simple, private way to track available cash, income, bills, expenses, and history without connecting a bank account or creating a login.

View on Google Play View Privacy Policy

What it does

SpendGuard helps answer one everyday money question:

How much can I safely spend before my next paycheck?

Status: Published on Google Play
Account required: No
Bank connection required: No
Storage: Local device storage

Simple budgeting between paychecks.

SpendGuard focuses on the money you have available right now and the bills coming up before your next payday.

Track Available Cash

Manually update your available cash so the app reflects what you actually have on hand.

Income and Payday View

Add income sources, choose a primary income, and view your next pay date.

Bills Before Payday

See which bills are due before your next paycheck and mark them paid as you go.

Expense Tracking

Log everyday spending like food, gas, shopping, or other expenses.

History

Review a history of cash updates, expenses, income, and paid bills.

Privacy-First

No required account, no bank connection, and budgeting data is stored locally on your device.

Built for clarity, not complexity.

SpendGuard is designed for quick checks, manual control, and simple decisions.

SpendGuard Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

SpendGuard is developed by Tiny Rebellion Studios. This privacy policy explains how SpendGuard handles information when you use the app.

No Account Required

SpendGuard does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide a username, password, email address, or profile information to use the app.

No Bank Connection

SpendGuard does not connect to your bank. The app does not collect banking usernames, banking passwords, account numbers, routing numbers, card numbers, or bank login information.

Local Device Storage

Budgeting information you enter into SpendGuard is stored locally on your device. This may include income sources, bill names, bill amounts, due dates, expenses, notes, budget history, app settings, and available cash amounts.

Tiny Rebellion Studios does not receive or store your manually entered budgeting information on its own servers.

Ads

SpendGuard may display banner ads through Google AdMob. AdMob and related Google services may collect or use device, advertising, diagnostic, or usage-related information according to Google's own policies.

SpendGuard does not share your manually entered budgeting information with advertisers.

In-App Purchases

SpendGuard may offer a one-time Remove Ads purchase through Google Play Billing. Purchases are handled by Google Play. Tiny Rebellion Studios does not receive your full payment card number or banking information.

Data Deletion

Because SpendGuard stores budgeting information locally on your device, you can delete app data by clearing the app's storage through your Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

No Selling Personal Information

Tiny Rebellion Studios does not sell your personal information.

Children's Privacy

SpendGuard is intended for general budgeting use and is not directed toward children. The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as SpendGuard changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
