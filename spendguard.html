Privacy

Your budget stays on your device.

SpendGuard does not require a user account or a connection to your bank. Budgeting information is stored locally on the device so you remain in control of the information you enter.

◈
Designed differently

Manual control without unnecessary access.

SpendGuard does not need:

  • A bank connection
  • A budgeting account
  • Your banking password
  • Cloud storage for your budget
  • Automatic access to your transactions

You stay in control.

SpendGuard uses the information you manually provide to calculate your available balance and daily safe-spending amount.

This approach requires a little more input from the user, but it avoids giving another service direct access to sensitive financial accounts.
