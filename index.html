Privacy

Privacy is the rule.

Privacy is the most important principle at Tiny Rebellion Studios. Whenever possible, our tools are designed to work without unnecessary accounts, logins, cloud storage, tracking, or personal data collection.

SpendGuard, for example, stores budgeting information locally on the user's device. The goal is simple: useful software that respects the person using it.
