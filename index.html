Tiny Rebellion Studios

Privacy-first apps, Excel tools, and game projects.

Mission

Tiny Rebellion Studios creates practical, high-quality software that helps people solve everyday problems while respecting their privacy. Technology should work for the user — not the other way around.

Our products are designed to be simple, reliable, and transparent. We collect only what is necessary and keep users in control of their own data whenever possible.

Projects

  • SpendGuard — Paycheck-focused budgeting app that helps users know what is safe to spend before their next paycheck.
  • Automated Project Tracker — Excel/VBA productivity workbook for organizing projects, tasks, notes, and progress.
  • Cash After Tax Tracker — Excel workbook for tracking income, expenses, and tax planning.
  • OffSync — Schedule-matching app that helps people find shared days off.
  • State of Dissent — Game project currently in development.

Privacy

Privacy is the most important rule at Tiny Rebellion Studios. Whenever possible, our tools are built to avoid unnecessary accounts, logins, cloud storage, or personal data collection.

SpendGuard stores budgeting information locally on the user's device.
